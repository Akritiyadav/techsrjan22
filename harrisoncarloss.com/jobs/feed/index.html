]]> Full-time | Festival Park | Staffordshire

We’re looking for an awesome and talented Front End Web Developer to join us. Yeah we know it’s exciting, so what are you waiting for?

A few things you probably need to know… You ideally need a minimum of 1-2 years development experience in an agency environment or as a seasoned freelancer, a passion for the latest web techniques and ideas to push the team forward.

Skills to pay the bills

  • A strong experience in HTML5 and CSS3/SASS, JavaScript and jQuery
  • Development of extended functionality for CMS systems (i.e WordPress)
  • Knowledge of Adobe CC (Photoshop, XD)
  • Accurately translate web designs into elegant digital experiences
  • Ability to work in an agile development environment
  • Self-motivated, organised, detail orientated and willing to be challenged
  • Excellent communication skills (oral and written)
  • A good understanding of SEO best-practice
  • Good problem solver and logical thinker
  • Strong understanding of UX/UI and cross-browser compatibility

It would be awesome (and we’d really love you!) if you also have any of the desirable skills we’re looking for…

  • Experience with cPanel and source control (e.g. GIT)
  • Experience with PHP frameworks (e.g. Laravel)
  • Experience with MySQL
  • Experience with e-commerce development (e.g. WooCommerce)
  • Experience with HubSpot
  • Familiarity with email marketing platforms (e.g. Mailchimp, Campaign Monitor)
  • Ability to show thought leadership and learning in your field

What you’ll be doing

  • Development, testing and deployment of new websites, platforms and digital experiences
  • Maintenance and bug fixing of legacy websites
  • Development and maintenance of HTML email templates
  • General hosting and server support
  • Collaborate and work in the studio to deliver digital projects on time, on budget at the forefront of web innovation
  • Research new technologies / development methods to expand our offering to clients

What you’ll get

  • Competitive salary dependant on experience
  • Discretionary yearly bonus
  • 33 Days holiday (including bank holidays)
  • Creative & culture filled modern office
  • Relaxed working environment
  • Regular team events
  • Free Nespresso, tea, coffee and fresh fruit
  • Job growth & role development
  • Be part of a growing and multi-skilled team
  • Bupa Health Expenses Plan
  • Death in Service cover
  • Company Pension
